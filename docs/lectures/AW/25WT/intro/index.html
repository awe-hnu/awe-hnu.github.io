Motivation

New theorizing pushes boundaries, expands frontiers, and breaks with tradition. As such, IS theory and theory-based papers, which includes empirical papers, are notoriously difficult to write. Breaking with tradition means that underlying assumptions, previously taken for granted, must now be revisited. Baird (2021)
