Exercise ‚Äî Level-up

Read following article ‚ÄúEveryone is above average‚Äù (Mollick 2023).

Exercise ‚Äî Fairspeech

Have a look at the fairspeech website (and at the research paper (Koenecke et al. 2020)).

Take 20 minutes, think about the following questions and prepare answers.

  • What is the issue with current automated speech recognition (ASR) systems ?
  • Which ethical issues do they cause?
  • What should be done to mitigate the risks?

Case Study

Form groups of two, take 20 minutes to revisit the case study and work on following questions.

  • Do you think that we will see something like artificial general intelligence in our lifetime?
  • Which of the aforementioned risks worries you the most? Why?
  • What is your opinion on the design of the OpenAI organization regarding its ability to deal responsibly with AI?
  • Do you think the self-regulation approaches taken by organizations like OpenAI, Microsoft, Meta, and Google are sufficient? Why (not)?
  • What do you think about making LLM and other AI technologies available via open source?
  • What are benefits and risks of legal regulations regarding the design and use of AI?

Let‚Äôs do some research to learn more about government and private sector-driven approaches to moving toward responsible AI.

European Union

Take 25 minutes to research about the Artificial Intelligence Act of the European Union.

Try to understand the risk-based approach and provide a short summary, also tackling following questions:.

  • What are high risk AI systems?
  • Which requirements should be mandatory requirements for these?

Prepare a short presentation (approx 5 minutes).

Microsoft

Research about Microsoft Responsible AI Standard (v2)

Take 25 minutes to understand the goals of this standard and the principles applied.

Also consider how Microsoft propose to assess the impact of AI and how the standard affects Microsofts own operations and services.

Prepare a short presentation (approx 5 minutes).

Google

Research about Googles AI principles.

Take 25 minutes to understand and to assess Googles principles. Create a short summary of your learnings, also tackling following questions:.

  • What kind of AI applications does Google not develop on principle? Are the statements credible?
  • Which practices do they recommend when developing AI?

Prepare a short presentation (approx 5 minutes).
