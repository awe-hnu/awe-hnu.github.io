Assignment

Your task is to introduce and explain a theory related to leadership or organizational behavior, present recent empirical findings and critically discuss the value of the theory for leadership in practice.

The presentation should be at min 12 minutes and at max 15 minutes. Please also prepare for questions at the end of the presentation (min 5 minutes).

Grading

An excellent presentation has the following characteristics

  • Depth and breadth of the topic is explored.
  • The presentation is based on scientific literature, which lends credibility to the presenter.
  • The remarks are supported by recent empirical findings.
  • The audience can relate theory to leadership challenges to gain a deeper understanding
  • Visual aids are carefully selected, enhance the presentation, illustrate the relevance and timeliness of the topic for the audience.
  • The presenter is confident and easy to understand
  • The audience is engaged in an informative and entertaining way.
  • The time was effectively and economically used.

Literature search

There are several ways of searching for relevant scientific literature. I recommend using the Web of Science or Google Scholar search engines.

Additional recommendations

  • Use the citation count as primary quality indicator (rule of thumb: the more cited, the more significant is the paper).
  • For understanding the theory try to follow the conversation (start with early, introductory papers and then read more recent papers).
  • Look for literature reviews that summarize the current state of knowledge.
  • For finding recent empirical evidence look for papers that apply the theory.
