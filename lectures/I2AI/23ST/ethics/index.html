Case Study

Ethical Implications of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Big Data

Tasks

Revisit the case study and work on following questions.

  • How do the problems described in the case study relate to what you have learned in this course about AI and how it works?
  • Are there statements in the paper that you disagree with and/or that are counterintuitive in light of current developments?
  • Regarding the AI services you use, where do you see the conditions of a informed consent as outlined in the case study fulfilled?
  • What are explainable models and why are they important from an ethical perspective?
  • What are problems concerning explainable models and deep neural networks?
  • Do you think we need more regulations to address AI and its impacts in the same way that we already do with climate change and biotechnology?

  • What are the principles described by the standard?
  • How does Microsoft propose to assess the impact of AI?
  • How does the standard affect Microsofts own operations and services?
