# Open questions

Resolve before writing new content for a new course or unit.

1. **Learning-objectives style.** SPM ("should have a solid understanding of") or DL ("will be able to:"). Per-course decision.
2. **Dual-format approach.** SPM (notes-primary) is the default. Choose DL (content-visible gating) for courses with heavy flipped-classroom design (many warm-ups, agendas, latticework check-ins).
3. **`categories:` YAML field.** SPM uses `categories: ["Lecture Notes"]`; DL omits it. Match the course convention.
4. **Language.** English by default. For German-medium courses (DE1, etc.), all body text including notes must be in German; do not assume English.
